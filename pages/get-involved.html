What we Do

As a local non-profit, Renegades actively seeks to build a community of normal people doing extraordinary things. By helping people believe that they can accomplish difficult things we instill passion for the outdoors and support a positive life style. Becoming a part of the community and learning to improve, they become the best they can be. By sponsoring events, leading campaigns and promoting community activities we bring about the Renegade passion in everyone.
